Zato is starting - the first boot creates a complete quickstart environment,
which takes a few minutes. Watch the progress with:

    kubectl logs -f deployment/{{ include "zato.fullname" . }}

Once the pod is ready, check the server:

    kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "zato.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.ports.server }}
    curl http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.ports.server }}/zato/ping

Dashboard:

    kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "zato.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.ports.dashboard }}

    http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.ports.dashboard }}
    Username: admin
    Password: the one from your {{ include "zato.passwordSecretName" . }} Secret
